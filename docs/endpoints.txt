/app
The root endpoint: Prompts user to choose task

/app/physical
Gives the list of physical tasks for the day

/app/physical/task1
Gives a description of the first physical task of the day

/app/physical/task2
Gives a description of the second physical task of the day

/app/physical/task3
Gives a description of the third physical task of the day

/app/mental
Gives the list of mental tasks for the day

/app/mental/task1
Gives the list of first mental task of the day

/app/mental/task2
Gives the list of second mental task of the day

/app/mental/task3
Gives the list of third mental task of the day
